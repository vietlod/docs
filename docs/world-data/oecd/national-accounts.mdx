# OECD National Accounts

OECD provides detailed national accounts following SNA 2008 methodology.

## Key Indicators

### Quarterly National Accounts
- GDP and components
- Volume and price indices
- Seasonally adjusted data

### Annual National Accounts
- Detailed expenditure breakdown
- Sector accounts
- Capital formation

## Coverage

| Data Type | Frequency | Countries |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| GDP | Quarterly | 38 |
| Expenditure | Annual | 38+ |
| Sector accounts | Annual | 38+ |

---

:::info Methodology
Follows SNA 2008 / ESA 2010 standards.
:::
