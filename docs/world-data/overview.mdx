# World Economic Data Overview

Welcome to **ECODATA World Data** - your comprehensive gateway to international economic statistics from major global organizations.

## Available Data Sources

ECODATA provides access to data from the following international organizations:

| Organization | Code | Description | Coverage |
|--------------|------|-------------|----------|
| **World Bank** | WB | Development indicators, debt statistics, microdata | 200+ countries |
| **IMF** | IMF | Financial statistics, trade, balance of payments | 190+ countries |
| **United Nations** | UN | Trade, demographics, SDG indicators | 193 member states |
| **OECD** | OECD | National accounts, industry, trade | 38 member countries |
| **Asian Development Bank** | ADB | Asian economic indicators, integration data | 49 member countries |
| **ILO** | ILO | Labor statistics, employment, wages | 180+ countries |
| **WTO** | WTO | Trade in goods and services | 164 member states |

## Key Data Categories

### Macroeconomic Indicators

- **GDP** - Gross Domestic Product (current USD, constant prices, growth rates)
- **Inflation** - Consumer Price Index, Producer Price Index
- **Interest Rates** - Policy rates, lending rates, deposit rates
- **Exchange Rates** - Official rates, real effective exchange rates

### Trade & Investment

- **Exports/Imports** - Merchandise trade, services trade
- **FDI** - Foreign Direct Investment inflows/outflows
- **Current Account** - Balance of payments components
- **Trade Partners** - Bilateral trade statistics

### Demographics & Social

- **Population** - Total, growth rate, age structure
- **Labor Force** - Employment, unemployment rates
- **Education** - Enrollment rates, literacy
- **Health** - Life expectancy, health expenditure

### Financial Indicators

- **Stock Markets** - Market capitalization, trading volumes
- **Banking** - Credit to private sector, NPL ratios
- **Debt** - External debt, government debt
- **Reserves** - Foreign exchange reserves

## How to Access Data

<Steps>

1. **Select Data Source** - Choose from World Bank, IMF, UN, or other sources
2. **Browse Indicators** - Navigate through categories to find specific indicators
3. **Select Countries** - Filter by country or region
4. **Choose Time Period** - Select years or date range
5. **Export Data** - Download in CSV, Excel, or JSON format

</Steps>

## Data Quality

All data provided through ECODATA maintains the following standards:

- ✅ **Official Sources** - Data directly from international organizations
- ✅ **Regular Updates** - Synchronized with source publication schedules
- ✅ **Metadata Included** - Full indicator definitions and methodologies
- ✅ **Cross-Referenced** - Validated against multiple sources where possible
