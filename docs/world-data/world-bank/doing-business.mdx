# Doing Business Indicators

The **Doing Business** project measured regulations affecting business activity across economies until 2020.

:::caution Discontinued
The Doing Business report was discontinued in 2021. ECODATA provides historical data from 2003-2020.
:::

## Historical Indicators

| Topic | Indicators |
|-------|------------|
| Starting a Business | Procedures, time, cost, capital |
| Construction Permits | Procedures, time, cost |
| Getting Electricity | Procedures, time, cost |
| Registering Property | Procedures, time, cost |
| Getting Credit | Legal rights, credit information |
| Protecting Minority Investors | Disclosure, director liability |
| Paying Taxes | Payments, time, total tax rate |
| Trading Across Borders | Time and cost to export/import |
| Enforcing Contracts | Time, cost, quality |
| Resolving Insolvency | Time, cost, recovery rate |
