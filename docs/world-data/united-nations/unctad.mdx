# UNCTAD Statistics

**UNCTAD (UN Conference on Trade and Development)** provides data on trade, investment, and development.

## Key Databases

### FDI Statistics
- FDI inflows and outflows
- FDI stock
- Cross-border M&A

### Commodity Prices
- Monthly commodity price indices
- Free market prices
- Terms of trade

### Trade in Services
- Services exports and imports
- By service category
