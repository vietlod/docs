# United Nations Data Overview

The **United Nations** provides comprehensive data through multiple specialized agencies and databases.

## Available Databases

| Database | Agency | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **UNSD** | Statistics Division | National accounts, demographics |
| **Comtrade** | Statistics Division | International trade by commodity |
| **UNCTAD** | Trade & Development | FDI, technology, commodities |
| **SDG** | SDG Indicators | Sustainable Development Goals |

## Key Data Areas

### National Accounts (UNSD)
- GDP by expenditure and production
- National income and savings
- Capital formation

### Trade Data (Comtrade)
- Merchandise trade by HS code
- Bilateral trade flows
- Trade indices

### Development Data (UNCTAD)
- FDI statistics
- Technology and innovation
- Commodity prices

### SDG Indicators
- 17 Goals, 169 Targets
- 231 unique indicators
- Progress tracking
