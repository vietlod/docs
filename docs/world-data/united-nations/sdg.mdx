# Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Indicators

The **SDG Indicators Database** tracks progress towards the 2030 Agenda.

## The 17 Goals

| Goal | Title | Key Indicators |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | No Poverty | Poverty headcount, social protection |
| 2 | Zero Hunger | Undernourishment, food security |
| 3 | Good Health | Mortality rates, health coverage |
| 4 | Quality Education | Enrollment, literacy |
| 5 | Gender Equality | Women in leadership, violence |
| 6 | Clean Water | Water access, sanitation |
| 7 | Affordable Energy | Electricity access, renewables |
| 8 | Decent Work | GDP growth, unemployment |
| 9 | Industry & Innovation | Manufacturing, R&D |
| 10 | Reduced Inequalities | Income share, migration |
| 11 | Sustainable Cities | Housing, air quality |
| 12 | Responsible Consumption | Waste, recycling |
| 13 | Climate Action | Emissions, climate policies |
| 14 | Life Below Water | Marine protected areas |
| 15 | Life on Land | Forest area, biodiversity |
| 16 | Peace & Justice | Violence, corruption |
| 17 | Partnerships | ODA, trade, data |
