# Vietnam Economic Data Overview

Welcome to **ECODATA Vietnam Data** - your comprehensive gateway to Vietnam's economic statistics from official sources.

## Available Data Sources

| Source | Code | Description | Update Frequency |
|--------|------|-------------|------------------|
| **GSO** | GSO | General Statistics Office | Monthly/Quarterly |
| **Customs** | CUSTOMS | Vietnam Customs | Monthly |
| **VHLSS** | VHLSS | Household Living Standards Survey | Biennial |
| **PCI** | VCCI | Provincial Competitiveness Index | Annual |
| **Stock Market** | VN | HOSE, HNX, UPCOM | Real-time |
| **PMI** | PMI | Purchasing Managers Index | Monthly |

## Key Data Categories

### Macroeconomic Indicators

- **GDP** - Quarterly and annual GDP growth
- **CPI** - Consumer Price Index
- **IIP** - Index of Industrial Production
- **Retail Sales** - Total retail trade and services

### Trade & Investment

- **Import/Export** - Merchandise trade by commodity and partner
- **FDI** - Foreign Direct Investment by sector and country
- **Trade Balance** - Monthly trade statistics

### Demographics & Labor

- **Population** - Census and survey data
- **Labor Force** - Employment by sector
- **Wages** - Average income statistics

### Provincial Data

- 63 provinces and centrally-governed cities
- Provincial GDP and economic indicators
- Regional comparisons

### Stock Market

- VN-Index, HNX-Index, UPCOM
- Sector indices
- Company financials

## How to Access Data

<Steps>

1. **Select Data Source** - Choose GSO, Customs, Stock Market, etc.
2. **Browse Categories** - Navigate to specific indicator groups
3. **Select Time Period** - Choose years, quarters, or months
4. **Filter by Region** - National or provincial level
5. **Export Data** - Download in CSV, Excel, or JSON

</Steps>

## Data Quality

- ✅ **Official Sources** - Data from government agencies
- ✅ **Regular Updates** - Aligned with official publication schedules
- ✅ **Bilingual** - English and Vietnamese metadata
- ✅ **Historical Coverage** - Back to 2000 for most indicators
