# Vietnam Retail Trade Statistics

Retail sales of goods and consumer services revenue data from GSO.

## Key Indicators

| Code | Indicator | Frequency |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| GSO_RETAIL_M | Retail sales (current prices) | Monthly |
| GSO_RETAIL_M_GR | Retail growth (y-o-y) | Monthly |
| GSO_SERVICES_M | Consumer services revenue | Monthly |

Retail sales typically peak in:
- Lunar New Year (January/February)
- September (Back to school)
- December (Year-end)
