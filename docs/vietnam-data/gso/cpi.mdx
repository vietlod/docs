# Vietnam Consumer Price Index (CPI)

The **Consumer Price Index (CPI)** measures changes in the price level of consumer goods and services.

## Key Indicators

| Code | Indicator | Frequency |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| GSO_CPI_M | CPI index (2019=100) | Monthly |
| GSO_CPI_M_GR | CPI growth (y-o-y) | Monthly |
| GSO_CPI_FOOD | Food & beverages CPI | Monthly |
| GSO_CORE_CPI | Core CPI (excl. food, energy) | Monthly |

## CPI Structure (Weights)

| Category | Weight | 2023 Change |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Food & Beverages | 33.6% | +3.5% |
| Housing & Materials | 15.7% | +5.8% |
| Transport | 9.4% | -4.2% |
| Education | 6.2% | +7.8% |
| Healthcare | 5.3% | +1.2% |
| Other | 29.8% | +2.8% |
| **Total CPI** | **100%** | **+3.25%** |

## Inflation History

| Year | CPI Inflation | Core Inflation |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| 2020 | 3.23% | 2.31% |
| 2021 | 1.84% | 0.81% |
| 2022 | 3.15% | 2.59% |
| 2023 | 3.25% | 4.16% |
| 2024F | 3.5-4.0% | ~3.0% |

## Inflation Target

The State Bank of Vietnam targets inflation at **4-4.5%** annually.

:::info Methodology
CPI calculated based on a basket of 654 goods and services.
