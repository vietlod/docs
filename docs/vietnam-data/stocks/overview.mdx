# Vietnam Stock Market Overview

Vietnam's stock market consists of three trading boards operated by two exchanges.

## Market Structure

| Exchange | Board | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| **HOSE** | VN-Index | Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange - Main board |
| **HNX** | HNX-Index | Hanoi Stock Exchange - Main board |
| **HNX** | UPCOM | Unlisted Public Company Market |

## Market Statistics (2024)
