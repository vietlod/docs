# Provincial Competitiveness Index (PCI)

The **PCI** is the most comprehensive annual assessment of provincial economic governance and business environment in Vietnam.

## About PCI

- **Publisher:** VCCI & USAID
- **Started:** 2005
- **Coverage:** 63 provinces
- **Methodology:** Enterprise survey + administrative data

## PCI Components (10 Sub-indices)

| Component | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Entry costs | 10% | Starting a business |
| Land access | 10% | Land availability, security |
| Transparency | 10% | Access to information |
| Time costs | 10% | Regulatory compliance time |
| Informal charges | 10% | Corruption |
| Policy bias | 5% | Fair treatment |
| Proactivity | 10% | Government support |
| Private sector dev. | 10% | Business support services |
| Labor training | 10% | Workforce quality |
| Legal institutions | 15% | Dispute resolution |
