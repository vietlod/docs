# Vietnam Indices and Rankings

Key indices measuring economic competitiveness, governance, and business conditions.

## Available Indices

| Index | Full Name | Publisher | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **PCI** | Provincial Competitiveness Index | VCCI | Annual |
| **PAPI** | Provincial Governance Index | UNDP/CECODES | Annual |
| **PMI** | Purchasing Managers Index | S&P Global | Monthly |
| **ICT** | ICT Development Index | MIC | Annual |

## Overview

### PCI - Business Environment
Measures provincial economic governance and business environment across 63 provinces.

### PAPI - Public Administration
Measures public administration performance and citizen satisfaction.

### PMI - Manufacturing Health
Leading indicator of manufacturing sector conditions.

### ICT Index
Measures ICT development and digital transformation readiness.
