# Vietnam Survey Datasets

Major survey datasets for detailed microeconomic analysis.

## Available Surveys

| Survey | Full Name | Frequency | Sample |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| **VHLSS** | Vietnam Household Living Standards Survey | Biennial | 46,000 households |
| **VES** | Vietnam Enterprise Survey (GSO) | Annual | 500,000 enterprises |
| **WBG-ES** | World Bank Enterprise Survey | 5 years | 1,000 firms |

## Survey Coverage

### VHLSS Topics
- Income and expenditure
- Poverty and inequality
- Education and health
- Housing and assets
- Employment

### Enterprise Surveys
- Revenue and costs
- Employment
- Investment
- Business environment
- Access to finance
