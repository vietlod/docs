# Vietnam Customs Trade Data

Trade statistics from the **General Department of Vietnam Customs (Tổng cục Hải quan)**.

## Available Data

| Category | Description | Frequency |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Total trade | Aggregate exports/imports | Monthly |
| By commodity | HS code breakdown | Monthly |
| By partner | Bilateral trade | Monthly |
| By province | Regional trade | Monthly |
